FAQ

This FAQ section was built using questions submitted on the public Google Form.

It is being kept online for future reference.


QUESTION: Could [the 96-character string] contain more than than the seed phrase and password?

ANSWER: There is no redundant information; Beyond the seed phrase and password, this string does not encode any other data.


QUESTION: Are the words in the mnemonic phrase separated by spaces?

ANSWER: They are not necessarily separated, but the structure is designed in such a way that (if you've found the correct solution) you will know where each word starts and ends.


QUESTION: Are the words in the mnemonic phrase [present] on the BIP39 list?

ANSWER: Yes, all 12 words from the seed phrase are present in the English BIP39 list.


QUESTION: Did you pick the 12 word seed phrase or was it chosen at random by the wallet software?

ANSWER: I picked the 12-word seed phrase myself. If you're familiar with seed phrase generation, you will know that the twelfth word is restricted to a smaller set that depends on the other eleven words. But the first 11 words were chosen directly by me, with a direct purpose in the puzzle's solution.

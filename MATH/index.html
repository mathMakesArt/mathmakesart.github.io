MathMakesArt Token (MATH)

In October 2021 I deployed the MATH token, my own custom Tezos token with FA2-compatibility.
A total of 8950 tokens were distributed between 1115 different wallet addresses, to each user who has collected at least one of my NFTs on HicEtNunc in the 7 months between March and September 2021.

Every user received a minimum of 1 token. The maximum number of tokens received by any user in this first distribution is 321 tokens, to Twitter user @Anarchytect2.

The MATH contract itself is an instance of my ArtContracts SmartPy contract, updated for Granada and modified to best facilitate the planned uses of MATH.


DISCLAIMER

This token is not intended as a monetary reward in any way, and has no associated value or exchange rate.

I am offering zero promise of present or future performance with this MATH token airdrop.
MATH is only my own personal means of tracking supporters, and potentially offering further reward opportunities to the collectors who have supported me in the past.
While I can't prevent users from privately transacting or otherwise transferring their tokens, I would like to discourage participation by any users who are seeking the token as a financial opportunity.
The opportunity exists in the art itself, and in the community of my collectors who are now united under a single decentralized contract.
